Top Story

The Explosive Rift: Ruto vs Gachagua & DCP

Kenya’s political scene is dominated by the bitter fallout between President William Ruto and his former Deputy, Rigathi Gachagua, now leader of the Democracy for the Citizens Party (DCP). Once allies who won the 2022 election together, the duo turned into rivals after Gachagua’s 2024 impeachment, and the standoff now defines much of the political conversation ahead of 2027.

As the feud intensifies, DCP faces defections, internal drama, and growing pressure from Ruto’s political machine. Even so, Gachagua insists the party remains strong and capable of reshaping the next election. With 2027 drawing closer, the Ruto-Gachagua showdown has become one of Kenya’s loudest and highest-stakes political dramas.
