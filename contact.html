About Us

Helicopter TV is a dynamic television and media platform committed to delivering impactful news, engaging sports coverage, entertainment features, and community-centered stories. We aim to be a trusted voice that informs the public, highlights important moments, and gives audiences content that is both relevant and inspiring.

In addition to media coverage, we also have a church located in Langata, and we record our services to reach more people through our platform. This allows us to combine media excellence with spiritual impact, giving viewers access to both quality programming and faith-based content.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inform, educate, inspire, and uplift our audience through high-quality broadcasting. We are dedicated to covering stories that matter, promoting talent, supporting community voices, and creating media content that reflects truth, excellence, and purpose.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading and influential media platform known for trusted reporting, powerful storytelling, creative entertainment, and meaningful community and church coverage. We strive to grow into a channel that reaches lives locally and beyond with content that makes a difference.
